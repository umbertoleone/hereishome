How Many People Apply to Come to the U.S.A - 3rd Quarter 2018

The United States of America welcomes tens of thousands of people from around the world to visit, live and work in the U.S. The following graph visualization shows some types of application forms received and processed by the USCIS in the 3rd Quarter of 2018. Additionally this visualization digs deeper and shows applications status (approved, denied, or pending) and the base type of each application (Family, employment, humanitarian, etc.).
